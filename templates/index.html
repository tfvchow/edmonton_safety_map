Edmonton Safety Map

Use the filters below to explore where incidents were reported across Edmonton. The numbers show recorded occurrences only and should not be read as direct crime rates. EPS explains, "Occurrences in your neighbourhood can be both criminal and non-criminal. Not all police activity is in response to crime. Regardless of where you live, the potential for police activity exists." Select a data year and optionally filter by category or type. Hold Ctrl (PC) or Command (Mac) while clicking to choose more than one option.

See the Edmonton Police Service resources for detailed descriptions of the incident categories and types. If no category or type is selected, all incidents are shown.

Heads up: EPS says each incident is moved to the nearest intersection, so the points are only approximate. We match every record to a community using the city's neighbourhood boundaries.

Data comes from open EPS incident reports and City of Edmonton boundaries. This project is a fair use of those datasets. See the Edmonton Police Service Terms and Conditions of Use and the City of Edmonton Open Data Terms of Use.
